PS2 games will soon be available to download on PSP, according to a fresh rumour from the Official PlayStation Magazine.

The latest issue of OPM UK reports in its Rumour Machine section that "a new service launching soon" will see titles from the Metal Gear Solid 2 and Ico era going handheld.


So far, PSOne has been the only Sony home console to see its titles released for download on PSP.

Sony last month revealed why some PSOne classics will never make the transition - because they're too buggy or complicated to port.

We like the sound of a PS2 Classics PSN service a lot - with God Of War 2 and GTA: San Andreas at the top of our wishlist.
