这是我3个星期学习AMP的成果，CG鉴赏只是仿照了大大们的作品制作了个极其简易的伪鉴赏，音乐鉴赏也是极其简陋的分支展现，效果也没做，和大大们的作品的距离还很大，对于这个粗糙的习作，望大家海涵 。
制作这个版本，纯粹为了抛砖引玉，游戏里有着一些未知问题，语句的排版问题比较严重，例如I come home.会变成Icome home.我已经放弃重排了（文本是使用.dat直接提取的），ed的staff列表的脚本未找到，无奈只能我自己做了一个。对于这个移植习作，希望大家指正 ，如果遇到什么问题的话，欢迎来掌机开发者之家[url]www.yayabo.cn[/url]进行汇报
这是X-change系列的第一作，是2001年的老游戏了（日版为1997发售，英文为2001），没语音（至少我没找到有语音的版本）
X-change系列目前有正篇3部，外传2部，只有正篇1代是没语音的，其他几代是有全语音的（包括男主人公，和配角），希望大大们有感兴趣的
简介：摘自维基百科
The first game revolves around Takuya Aihara, a student specializing in chemistry, as he is transformed into a female in a small lab accident. The number of characters in the game is limited compared to the others and most of the storyline revolves around Asuka Katakiri and Asami Satoh, both of whom are students at his school.
The main character is Takuya Aihara, an average junior college student with one character flaw: he is a bit of a mental weakling. No matter how hard he tries, it seems that all the girls around him are constantly pushing him around: his teasing stepsister Natsumi, the genius president of his chemistry club Asami, and especially Asuka, his beautiful childhood friend.
One day, while cleaning some shelves in the chemistry room, he spills a mysterious chemical on himself. The next thing he knows, he is transformed into a girl. The game has seven different endings; in some Takuya remains a woman, in others he returns to being a man.
