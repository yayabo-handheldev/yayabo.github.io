NGP版《杀戮地带》由 Sony Cambridge 开发 Guerrilla Games表示没问题

Guerrilla Games not worried - Sony Cambridge has worked on Killzone before.

The development studio behind the NGP version of Killzone is Sony Cambridge, Guerrilla Games has told Gamerzines. The studio, which has previously worked on the PSP version of LittleBigPlanet, has been working with Guerrilla for a number of years on content for Killzone titles.

"Our **ter studio in Cambridge is doing that," said Guerrilla Games senior producer Steven Ter Heide. "We've been working with them very closely for a couple of years now. They've been helping us develop actual Killzone content for all the games."

"We feel like the game is in good hands. We'll still oversee it because the universe is something we've built up, we know the war and the rules that have been established, but they're driving the development."

Guerrilla Games 表示不担心 Sony Cambridge 以前就参与制作过《杀戮地带》的。

NGP版《杀戮地带》由Sony Cambridge 制作. Guerrilla Games 告知 Gamerzines. Sony Cambridge 工作室曾研发制作过PSP版小小大星球 并且和Guerrilla共事制作过《杀戮地带》系列。

Guerrilla Games 资深制作人Steven Ter Heide 告诉我们 “我们的姐妹制作室Cambridge正在着手于NGP版《杀戮地带》的制作，”
“我们一直和他们又着密切的工作关系 现在他们为整个游戏研发游戏内容”

“NGP版的我们觉得很棒的说 但我们仍将监督他们的工作 因为游戏背景设定是由我们构造的 我们清楚那种设定的战争和规则的确立 他们操刀制作。”

VideoGamer.com Analy**
While it's disappointing that high profile NGP titles aren't being developed by the studios that made the PS3 games, Sony does appear to have a strong pool of developers for its handheld. Sony Cambridge's LittleBigPlanet for PSP was in some ways even better than the PS3 original so Killzone appears to be in good hands.

虽然NGP版的没有PS3版的制作组来制作 不过SONY确实有着强大的开发者团队在手持设备上。Sony Cambridge 的 PSP版《小小大星球》在某些方面比PS3原版的更优秀，所以NGP版的《杀戮地带》似乎也将有更好的表现。
